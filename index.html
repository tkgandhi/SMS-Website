Springhouse Middle School

Springhouse Middle School(SMS)is a school in the Parkland School District. The school mascot is a Timberwolf, and provides education to grades 6, 7, & 8.


The School Motto is, "Educating for Success, Inspiring Excellence".

Academics

Students learn Math, Science, ELA, Social Studies, Info Tech, Family Consumer Science, Tech Ed, each day. They get homework for each subject, and have to take a STAR placement test for Math and ELA in the beginning, middle, and end of the school year to see their growth. They get Timberwolf Time every Wednesday & Thursday to work on extra assignments or to go to important meetings.
